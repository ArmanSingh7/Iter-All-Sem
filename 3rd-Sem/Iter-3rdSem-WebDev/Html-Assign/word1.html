Usage

  • Despite being offered help, his churlish response left everyone surprised and offended
  • Her churlish behavior at the party, where she refused to socialize and made negative comments, put a damper on the evening.
  • It was considered churlish of him not to thank the team for their hard work on the project.

Synonyms and Antonyms

Synonyms Antonyms
Rude Polite
Surly Courteous

Definition: Behaving or characterized by rudeness, surliness, or a lack of manners. It refers to a discourteous and ill-tempered demeanor, often marked by a failure to show proper respect or consideration for others, especially in social interactions. Churlish behavior is impolite, uncivil, or ungracious in nature..
